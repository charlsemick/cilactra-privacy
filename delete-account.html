Request Account Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data from Cilactra NG.

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1 (Recommended):

  • Open the Cilactra NG app
  • Go to Settings
  • Select Delete Account

Option 2 (Manual Request):

  • Email us at: support@empirewebservice.com
  • Use the subject: "Delete My Account"
  • Include your registered email or phone number

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile and personal details
  • Transaction and purchase history
  • Saved preferences and settings
  • Uploaded content (photos, profile data)

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited period where required for:

  • Legal and regulatory compliance
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Financial record-keeping obligations

Processing Time

All deletion requests are processed within 7 days from the date of request.
